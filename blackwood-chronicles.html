Civilization's Edge

Blackwood sits in the middle of the an ancient forest with mountain ranges on two sides. The climate can be cold and unforgiving but the land can produce massive wealth for those brave enough. There have been reports of a gathering Orc horde to the northwest as well as increased Gnoll numbers in the forests nearest Blackwood. The citizens of Blackwood are feeling anxious but the squabble of the Five Houses are the cause of that.

Arlo Holstaad

Setting

Blackwood is awash with disagreements between the Five Houses. House Slovin wishes to bring in BnB Trading company to increase supply, and demand, for the coveted wooden craft and art pieces made from the magical trees surrounding Blackwood. However, not all the house agree with this.

History

Blackwood was settled a few hundered year ago by Dress Ironhide. What brought Dress and his compatriots so far from civilization has been lost but his legend lives one. Wild beasts were rife in the area, including multiple Orc hordes that have constantly plagued the town for the few centuries since it's settlement.

Recent Past

In the time since the Five Houses: Kelda, Slove, Holstaad, Raas, and Brine each have a member that sits on the Council of Five. Times have been relatively good as of late, but with BnB Trading company coming into play the boat is starting to rock. There have also been rumors of growing numbers of Orv tribes, a mysterious cult kidnapping people and burning down towns.
